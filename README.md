### 💡 About My Journey

Driven by a passion for complex data, I transitioned into bioinformatics, specifically **Spatial Transcriptomics**, where I honed my skills in processing high-dimensional biological datasets.

Today, I channel all these interdisciplinary analytical skills into the frontiers of AI. I am fully dedicated to **Computer Vision (CV)** and **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLM)**. I believe my non-traditional path is my superpower, allowing me to approach AI research and problem-solving from fresh, multifaceted perspectives!
